Place your news article images in this folder.
File naming examples:
  olympiad.jpg         → for the Maths Olympiad article
  cultural-day.jpg     → for Cultural Day
  waec-results.jpg     → for WAEC results
  science-lab.jpg      → for Science Lab opening
  sports-day.jpg       → for Sports Day
  admissions.jpg       → for Admissions article

Then in src/data/site.js, update each article's image field:
  image: '/images/news/olympiad.jpg'

Recommended image size: 1200 × 700px (landscape)
